关于 ABOUT
ABOUT

我们关心上海的鸟，也关心在这里停留、离开、再回来的人。 通过散步、观察、录音和聊天，把看上去无关紧要的小事，慢慢积累成一座声音和记忆的档案馆。

We care about the birds of Shanghai, and also the people who stay, leave, and return. Through walking, watching, recording and conversations, we slowly turn seemingly trivial moments into an archive of sound and memory.

我们正在推进的事情 WHAT WE ARE DOING
WHAT WE ARE DOING
  • 筹备一档关于上海、鸟类与迁徙记忆的播客
  • 记录城市里人和鸟的相遇、错过与再见
  • 试验一张「声音地图」：用声音标记城市中的栖息地与流动路线
  • Preparing a podcast about Shanghai, birds and memories of migration
  • Recording encounters, missed meetings and reunions between people and birds in the city
  • Experimenting with a “sound map” that marks habitats and routes of movement through sound
